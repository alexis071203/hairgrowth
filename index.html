All About Hair

Wash Day!

    Do's
  • Use a little because it goes a long way
  • Use the pads of your fingers to wash your hair
  • Choose products that works for YOUR hair
  • Wash when you think it's needed
  • Deep condition when you think it's needed
  • Use your fingers as a comb
  • When using shampoo, focus on the scalp
  • When done with shampoo, make sure all of it is out before moving on
  • When conditioning, let it sit in your hair for 5-10 minutes *the longer doesn't change the results
  • When washing out conditioner, USE cold water to sink in the moisture
  • If you choose to go to a salon, find someone who knows how to treat your hair with care.
    Dont's
  • Use a comb when washing. It will cause unnecessary breakage
  • Scratch scalp with fingernail
  • Follow other people's wash day routine
  • rush through
  • shampoo after everytime you sweat
  • tug on hair; pulling on wet hair could cause you to lose a few strands
  • wash your hair a lot

Use of Heat

  • Avoid using heat on unclean hair
  • Do not blow dry on sopping wet hair
  • When blow drying, don't hold the blow dryer too close to your hair
  • Allow your hair to dry 75% before blow drying
  • Properly detangle hair before blow drying and straightening the hair
  • Use a heat protectant before applying any type of heat
  • Avoid using heat daily
  • Sectioning your hair is important

    • Protective Styling

  • Don't go too tight, too small, or too long on protective styling
  • Always moisture your hair while protective styling
  • Braids, twist-outs, and braid-outs are good protective styles
  • You don't have to go all out
  • Protective styling is not the only way to grow your hair
  • Avoid leaving a protective styling in too long, it could led to breakage or hair loss
